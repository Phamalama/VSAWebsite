WHAT HAPPENED IN CENTRAL VIETNAM?

The 2020 Central Vietnam floods were a series of floods in Central Vietnam which also affected some areas in Cambodia and Laos in October and early November 2020. The floods focus heavily in several provinces including Thừa Thiên Huế, Hà Tĩnh, Quảng Bình, Quảng Trị, and Quảng Ngãi.
