VIETNAMESE STUDENT ASSOCIATION

At the University of Cincinnati

VSA is a member organization of the umbrella group UVSA-Midwest. Our mission and purpose is to create an open and welcoming environment for those interested in the Vietnamese culture and for those that are looking to be leaders. We focus primarily on interactive and informative general body meetings, whether casual or formal. We emphasize collaboration with our fellow Asian-Interest organizations on UCâ€™s campus. We promote OPEN discussion on social/racial matters, especially within the Vietnamese, Vietnamese-American community. Annually, our group hosts Mid-Autumn Festival, a Vietmamese/Chinese celebration to celebrate harvest, where a variety of performances take place.
